**Q1. What is the average salary of a data scientist in India?**
It depends on experience, but entry level is around 4L to 8L per year. Senior roles can go up to 15L - 30L+.

**Q2. Why do salaries differ between cities?**
Mainly because the cost of living is different. Cities like Mumbai have high rent so the salary is adjusted for that.

**Q3. Which skills increase salary in data science?**
Having skills in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, GenAI, and cloud like AWS or Azure really helps increase the package.

**Q4. Why are data scientists in high demand today?**
They help businesses predict trends, reduce risk, and make more profits so companies want them.

**Q5. How does experience affect salary in data science?**
More experience simply means more money. A fresher might get 5L but a 3 year experienced pro can get 10L.


Summary of learnings:
* Data science is a high paying tech career that uses programing and stats to solve problems.
* The starting pay is usually 4L-8L and grows with more experience.
* Top cities like Bangalore and Mumbai pays more due to living expenses.
* Learning advance things like AI, ML and Big data is the key to get higher salaries.
